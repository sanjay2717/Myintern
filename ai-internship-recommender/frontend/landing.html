Process

Simple. Fast. Accurate.

Four inputs. Five results. Zero confusion. Designed specifically for first-generation learners and candidates from rural and remote backgrounds.

Step 01
📋

Enter Your Profile

Select your education level, sector of interest, preferred state, and any skills you have. No resume required. No account needed.

Step 02
🤖

AI Matches Your Profile

Our TF-IDF recommendation engine analyses your inputs against 40 plus internship listings and computes a personalised match score for each.

Step 03
📊

Review Your Matches

Receive 3 to 5 ranked internship cards showing the role, company, location, stipend, and your personal match percentage.

Step 04
🚀

Apply With Confidence

Choose the internship that fits best and apply directly on the PM Internship portal with informed, data-backed confidence.
